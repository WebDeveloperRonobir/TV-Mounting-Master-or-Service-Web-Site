What we care about !

At TV Mount Master, we are the trusted experts in wall-mounted TV installations, ensuring your peace of mind with secure TV installation and top-notch service quality.

    When you entrust us with mounting your TV on the wall, you're choosing professionals who recognize the significance of precise installation. We even provide the convenience of same-day service, making the process of pro TV mounting hassle-free. We give top priority to the safety of your home and take measures to safeguard your audio and visual equipment from any potential damage. Additionally, we ensure that your space looks even better than before, as we leave it neat and tidy.
    Safety is essential when securing your TV, especially if you have kids or guests. We fasten your flat screen securely, so accidents are prevented. Your investment deserves protection, and wall mounting ensures it's part of your home. Your pets' safety is our concern too!
    TV mounting can be stressful, but we take care of everything. Our experts handle the whole process, from attaching brackets to mounting on the wall. We find the right solutions, whether you need a swivel, tilt, or custom mount.
    Your well-being is our top concern. Our skilled technicians ensure your bracket is strong and safe. We also manage cables, keeping things organized.
    We give you detailed information about our services, so you can confidently choose us. With over ten years of experience, we can mount TVs on various surfaces, including drywall, brick, stone, tile, concrete, wood paneling, and exterior surfaces.
    We offer flexible mounting locations, including interior walls, above the fireplace, hanging from the ceiling, and even in exterior areas. We're experts at hiding wires, creating a clean look. We handle drywall repairs, texture matching, and painting.
